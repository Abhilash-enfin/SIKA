Frequently asked questions

Why should I use Sika Health to accept HSA/FSA payments?

Sika Health makes the entire process of accepting HSA/FSA payments simple and easy. When you install the Sika Health payment method, your customers can pay using their HSA/FSA debit cards right away.

I’m a healthcare company that processes HSA/FSA cards already today — what is Sika Health doing differently?

We work with many retailers who previously processed HSA/FSA cards on their own but made the switch to Sika for a more seamless customer experience and to adhere with the latest compliance guidelines. Specifically, companies billing HSA/FSA cards for anything other than check-ups and co-pays are required to implement and operate an inventory management system (IIAS). That requirement applies to retailers of glasses, sunscreens, hearing aids, exercise equipment, and more. In fact, the IRS recently called for audits of HSA/FSA purchases from healthcare service providers related to this very issue - See IRS Memorandum. With Sika, there's no need to change your Merchant Category Codes (MCC) to work around this — any business selling eligible items can compliantly accept these regulated funds through our platform.

How does Sika Health handle necessary compliance?

Compliance is the backbone of our offering. As a SIGIS member and Integrated Payment Provider, we help merchants navigate the latest industry standards to ensure proper certifications to accept HSA/FSA funds. When merchants compliantly accept HSA/FSA funds, their customers can shop with HSA/FSA cards without worrying about purchase eligibility. In the event that an HSA/FSA administrator requests additional substantiation, Sika will be there to support your customers.

I’ve heard that mattresses and massages are HSA/FSA eligible. Is that true?

From mattresses to skincare, supplements to fitness equipment, there’s a slew of products that shoppers can purchase with their HSA/FSA funds. Sika Health’s team of medical and legal experts work with appropriate authorities to determine the eligibility of the products in your portfolio on an ongoing basis. This is all part of our offering.

Let’s do this. How easy is it to integrate with Sika Health?

Sika Health works with many e-commerce platforms out of the box such as Shopify and WooCommerce. If you have a Custom checkout, we offer white-glove installation as well. Whatever your needs, we will help you be successful.

Why was my HSA/FSA transaction declined when I tried to purchase an item marked “HSA/FSA accepted”?

Sika and its partner retailers endeavor to provide high transaction authorization rates for HSA and FSA shoppers. However, some HSA and FSA plans are not currently configured to accept charges from certain retailers based on the retailers’ Merchant Category Code, meaning that cards issued by those plans may be declined for eligible transactions. Sika is working with plan administrators to address this for a better customer experience. In the meantime, please pay for your purchase with your debit or credit card and use your receipt to file for reimbursement.
